Oh Shit!

2005, Group project with collaborators Jenks Whittenburg, Donnie Bugdon and Prithvi Virasinghe

Summary

Oh Shit! is a narrative-based board game about the stresses of buying gifts for loved ones for the holidays, while being sick from eating bad food. The player who is done buying all the gifts on their list first is the winner. Please excuse the potty humor!
